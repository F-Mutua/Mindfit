Study & Wellness Tips

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After four sessions, take a longer break.

Mindful Breathing

When stressed, try the 4-7-8 technique: inhale for 4s, hold for 7s, exhale for 8s.

Active Recall

Test yourself on the material rather than passively rereading it for better retention.
